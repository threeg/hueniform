Banner text is the error envelope's message, shown verbatim. The three possible rejections here:
· 400 unsupported_format — “Only JPEG, PNG and WebP photographs are accepted.”
· 413 file_too_large — “That photograph is larger than 20 MB — please use a smaller image.”
· 400 unreadable_image — “That file could not be read as an image — it may be corrupted.”
